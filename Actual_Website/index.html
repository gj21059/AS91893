nā Johnny Waititi

Rangitoto te maunga
Waitematā te moana
Ngāti Paoa te iwi e
Ko Pupuke te roto
Anei ngā kura o Te Kāhui Ako
E ngā iwi, tēnā koutou (2x)

Rangitoto is the mountain
Waitemata is the ocean
Ngati Paoa are the iwi
Pupuke is the lake
